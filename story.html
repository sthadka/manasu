From Manasi

At first, I thought maybe it could be the weather , time, day or the place that made us feel we are perfect for each other. But when I now think over it, neither of them played any part ... the weather may be to an extent because it made our conversation more euphonious.

I have heard "it is all about two people meeting at the right time" but I feel, if two people make the time right, that is when the spark ignites and you just know he/she is the right one. That is how I found my soul mate in Sukumar may be. He came across as an astute, sometimes jocular and a very articulate person and all our imperfections seemed perfect. Throughout the meeting, he just made my ocean of complicated thoughts about "my kinda guy", come out as smooth waves one by one and by the end of the meeting I was certain that he is the kind of guy I am looking for.

Our weird and crazy perceptions about life, our interests couldn't have matched better. He is a little more crazy than me, he is definitely more knowledgeable, he has better understanding of things than me and many more things which I will find out once we are together. I hardly believe in luck, but this time I definitely got lucky :)
